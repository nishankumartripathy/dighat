AboutDight
International
Chambers

Dight International Chambers is a non-profit organization registered under the companies
Act of 2013 on 16th July 2019 with the primary objective of livelihood upliftment through mobilization
of relevant stake holders in India.
We are looking at transformation of 5,000,000 lives across India, through various programs by the year 2022.
Our efforts coupled with volunteering efforts from motivated people can certainly yield still bigger
masses under the coverage of targeted programs..
